About Us

Founded in 2014, ECSOL Ecological Solutions Co. is an Egyptian shareholding company specialized in engineering, procurement and construction services for medium-scale projects in Egypt. With a proven track record, high technical capabilities and a diversified portfolio, ECSOL successfully executed multiple projects in various activities. Our expertise covers civil constructions, infrastructure, water and wastewater supplies, and electromechanical installation.
